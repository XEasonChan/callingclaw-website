Joins the meeting on time

Enters Google Meet or Zoom as a real participant. No manual launch, no reminders — it just shows up, ready to contribute.

  • Auto-join Meet & Zoom
  • Real participant with audio/video
  • Transparent AI identity

  • Records decisions & actions
  • Ships before you hang up
