🦞 Features Overview

A Meeting Room for Your OpenClaw

CallingClaw transforms your AI from a text-only assistant into a meeting participant. Talk to it directly — it sees your screen, hears your voice, and knows exactly what you're pointing at and where to fix it.

Meeting Prep

AI Reads the Docs, Prepares the Agenda, Then Joins

Before joining a meeting, OpenClaw reads relevant documents, analyzes the topic, and prepares structured discussion materials. It creates the Google Meet, joins with prepared context, and is ready to discuss — like a teammate who actually did the homework.

  • Reads MEMORY.md + project files to understand full context
  • Generates structured analysis: challenges, questions, talking points
  • Creates meeting, joins, and starts with prepared brief injected

  • Meeting ends with a complete, confirmed task list

Context Matching

Screenshots + Preferences, Matched in Real-Time

When you talk to OpenClaw in a meeting, CallingClaw captures what's on screen and matches it with what you're saying. It knows exactly what you're talking about and where the changes need to happen — then passes matched context to OpenClaw as executable tasks.

  • Screenshots auto-captured at key conversation moments
  • User preferences matched to specific UI elements
  • Matched pairs flow into OpenClaw's task queue
