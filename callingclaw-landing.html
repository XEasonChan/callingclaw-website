Privacy & Security

Your Meetings Stay Yours

💻

100% Local Processing

CallingClaw runs entirely on your Mac. The backend server is localhost — nothing routes through our servers.

🔐

Direct API Connections

Audio streams directly to your chosen AI provider (Gemini, Grok, or OpenAI) using your own API keys. We never see or store your data.

🚫

No Cloud Storage

Meeting notes, transcripts, and recordings stay on your local filesystem. Nothing is uploaded unless you explicitly choose to.

🔒

You Control the Keys

Bring your own API keys for Gemini, OpenAI, Grok, Claude (via OpenRouter), and Google Calendar. Revoke access anytime.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What meeting platforms are supported?

Google Meet and Zoom. CallingClaw joins as a real participant — no browser extensions or plugins needed.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 13 (Ventura) or later. Apple Silicon recommended for best performance. No virtual audio drivers needed — audio injection is handled at the browser level.

Do I need OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is optional. CallingClaw works standalone with just an API key (Gemini, Grok, or OpenAI). With OpenClaw, you get deeper features: project-aware meeting prep, deep reasoning, and the /callingclaw skill for hands-free meeting management.

What does it cost?

CallingClaw is free during beta. The main cost is your AI provider API usage. Gemini 3.1 Flash Live (default) costs ~$0.02/min, Grok ~$0.05/min, OpenAI ~$0.30/min. Claude via OpenRouter for deep analysis is extra.

Can other people in the meeting tell it's AI?

CallingClaw joins with its own participant name and speaks with an AI voice. It's transparent by design — we believe AI meeting participants should be clearly identifiable.

Can I customize the AI voice?

Yes. Choose from 3 providers (Gemini, Grok, OpenAI) with 25+ voice personas. Gemini offers Kore, Puck, Aoede and 5 more. Switch in the Desktop status bar or voice-test page.

Is my meeting data private?

Yes. CallingClaw runs 100% locally on your Mac. Audio is streamed directly to your AI provider. We never see, store, or process your meeting data. Learn more →
